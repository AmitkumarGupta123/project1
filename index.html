Important Points About A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

1.Here are some important points about Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, the 11th President of India: 2. Missile Man: He was known as the "Missile Man of India" for his work on ballistic missiles and launch vehicles. 3. Nuclear tests: He played a key role in India's 1998 Pokhran-II nuclear tests, which made India a nuclear-armed nation 4. Space and missile programs: He was a leader in India's space and missile programs. 5. Education: He inspired many people with his passion for education. 6. Books: He wrote Ignited Minds: Unleashing the Power Within India, which focused on the potential of India's youth. 7. President: He was elected President of India in 2002 and was the country's first bachelor president. 8. Technical advisor: He worked as a Technical Advisor in the Indian Ministry of Defense in 1992 9. Honorary doctorates: He received honorary doctorates from 40 universities. 10.Vegetarian: He was a pure vegetarian and never ate meat.
